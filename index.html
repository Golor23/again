Welcome to PESO (Mataas na Kahoy)

The Public Employment Service Office provides free employment and training assistance to jobseekers and employers.

About PESO

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) is a government-funded agency that assists citizens in finding employment opportunities and provides counselling, training, and career guidance.

Mission

To provide accessible job placement services, promote the welfare of the workforce, and support economic development in the community.
